If you created an account in Lunaria, you may request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

To delete your Lunaria account and data, choose one of the following options:

Option 1 (Recommended):

What data is deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data is permanently deleted:

  • User account (email and authentication data)
  • Menstrual cycle records
  • Daily logs (symptoms, mood, notes)
  • Profile and preferences
  • Cloud-synced data

Data retention

  • Account and personal data are deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymous diagnostic data, if any, may be retained for legal or security purposes
  • Data stored locally on your device can be deleted by uninstalling the app

Guest users

  • Uninstalling the app deletes all data
