1. Introduction

Welcome to Lunaria ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our menstrual cycle tracking application.

By using Lunaria, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Profile Information: Name, date of birth
  • Health Information:
    • Menstrual cycle data (cycle type, average cycle length, period length)
    • PCOS/PCOS-related information (diagnosis, symptoms, treatments)
    • Contraceptive method
    • Pregnancy intentions
  • Daily Tracking Data:
    • Symptoms
    • Flow intensity (light, medium, heavy)
    • Mood
    • Personal notes

2.2 Authentication Information

  • Email address (if you choose to create an account)
  • Google account information (if you choose to sign in with Google)
  • Password (encrypted and stored securely)

2.3 Device Information

  • Device identifiers
  • Operating system information
  • App version
  • Push notification tokens (if you enable notifications)

2.4 Media

  • Profile pictures (if you choose to upload one)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: To provide menstrual cycle tracking, predictions, and calendar features
  • Personalization: To customize your experience based on your cycle patterns and preferences
  • Health Insights: To generate predictions about your menstrual cycle, fertile windows, and ovulation periods
  • Notifications: To send you reminders and alerts (only if you opt-in)
  • Account Management: To manage your account and authenticate your identity
  • Data Synchronization: To sync your data across devices (only if you choose to create an account)
  • Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

4. Data Storage and Architecture

4.1 Local-First Architecture

Lunaria uses a local-first architecture, meaning:

  • All your data is stored locally on your device using SQLite
  • The app works completely offline
  • Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to sync

4.2 Optional Cloud Sync

  • Cloud synchronization is completely optional
  • You can use Lunaria as a guest user without creating an account
  • If you choose to create an account, your data will be synced to our secure cloud server (PocketBase) to enable access across multiple devices
  • You can disable cloud sync at any time in settings
  • You can choose your sync frequency (daily, weekly, or monthly)

4.3 Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you delete the app or manually delete the data
  • Cloud Data: Stored until you delete your account or request data deletion
  • You can export your data at any time using the export feature in settings

5. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

5.1 No Sale of Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

5.2 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • PocketBase: Used for optional cloud storage and authentication. PocketBase is a self-hosted backend service that stores your data securely.
  • Google Authentication: If you choose to sign in with Google, Google's authentication service is used. Please review Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Expo Services: We use Expo's services for notifications, image picking, and app functionality. These services process data according to their respective privacy policies.

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: Data transmitted to our servers is encrypted using secure protocols
  • Local Storage: Data stored locally is protected by your device's security features
  • Access Controls: Cloud data is protected by authentication and access controls
  • Regular Updates: We regularly update our security measures

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can access all your data through the app
  • Export: You can export your data in JSON format at any time
  • Delete: You can delete your account and all associated data
  • Correction: You can update or correct your information at any time
  • Opt-Out: You can disable notifications and cloud sync at any time
  • Guest Mode: You can use the app without creating an account (data stays local)

8. Children's Privacy

Lunaria is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in countries other than your country of residence. By using Lunaria, you consent to the transfer of your data to these countries.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you a notification (if you have notifications enabled)

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at:

Email: lunaria.support@luismartinez.site
